TABLETOP SNOOKER

A local two-player snooker frame with aiming, power control, fouls, scoring and proper colour order.

FRAME PAUSED

HOW TO PLAY

Pot a red for 1 point, then nominate and pot a colour. Colours return to their spots while reds remain.

After the final red, clear yellow, green, brown, blue, pink and black in order.

A legal pot keeps your turn. Missing or fouling passes play to the other player. Fouls award at least 4 points.

Tap or drag around the table to aim, set the power, then press Take Shot.
